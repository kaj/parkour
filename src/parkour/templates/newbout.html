How to get started

Log onto the system as one of the programmers in the pair. Log out.

Log onto the system as the other programmer in the pair. Select course, lab and enter your pair programming partner's name in the text field.

How to stop pair programming

Remember to click pause at the end of a pair programming session, otherwise the system will keep logging over lunch, over night or even over the weekend!
